Directions for play:

1. Open group_9_assignment7.pde in Processing.
2. Run the file.
3. Your character is the hot pink circle. You control the direction that you move with the WASD keys. 
4. If you overlap a circle smaller than you, you will consume the mass of that circle and become bigger. If a circle larger than you overlaps you, it will consume your mass, and you will lose the game.
5. If you consume every other circle in the game, you win the game.
6. Click the "p" key at any time to pause or unpause the game. Click the left mouse button at any time to restart the game.
